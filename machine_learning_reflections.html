Reflections

Undestanding the business probelem and if it can be solvec by Machine Learning (ML) is important; we do not want to solve for problem that does not exist or even solve it with the wrong too. Another core component is the data and it will make or break the project, having quality data is important to creating ML models that have little or no bias at all. Building Machine Learning models is not that simple and there are various aspects that must be kept in mind irrespective of the technologies being used. When ML models are used even in simple scenarios like contet generation, it is important that care is taken to that no intellectual property issues arise. The legal implications could be grave for the user. In more sensitive sectors like health, microscopic care must be taken because the implications could be dangerous. It is important to bake in evaluations and explainability mechanisms that make sure the model are safe for use in wider society.
